Welcome

I am a PhD student in Economics at the University of Glasgow. My research interests include labour economics, education economics, applied econometrics, and causal inference.

My current research examines the effects of taking a gap year before higher education on early-career labour market outcomes.

Research

The Cost of a Gap Year: Education Delay and Career Outcomes
This paper studies the association between taking a gap year before entering higher education and early-career earnings.

Exact Parametric Causal Mediation Analysis with a Categorical Mediator
This project develops closed-form expressions for causal mediation analysis with nominal categorical mediators.
